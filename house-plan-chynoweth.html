About The Chynoweth Plan

The Chynoweth Plan offers a refreshing take on modern living with its clean lines, expansive windows, and thoughtful layout designed for comfort and functionality. The striking front facade welcomes you into a home where open spaces and natural light are paramount. The main floor is an entertainer's delight, featuring a gourmet kitchen with a large island, a spacious dining area, and a cozy living room that seamlessly connects to outdoor living spaces.

Upstairs, the private bedrooms provide serene retreats, including a luxurious master suite with a generous walk-in closet and a spa-like ensuite. An additional well-appointed bedroom upstairs as well as several optional basement bedrooms offer flexible ample space for family and guests. The finished basement extends the home's living area, perfect for a media room, home gym, or an additional guest suite. The Chynoweth Plan combines elegance with practicality, making it an ideal choice for families seeking a stylish and adaptable home.

  • Square Footage: Approximately 4500 sq ft
  • Bedrooms: 4 (+ optional basement bedrooms)
  • Bathrooms: 3.5 (+ optional basement bathrooms)
  • Features: Modern design, open-concept living, gourmet kitchen, large windows, master suite with spa bath, bonus room, finished basement, 3-car garage.
