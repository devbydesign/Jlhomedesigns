About The Chynoweth Plan

The Chynoweth Plan presents a beautifully designed rambler with a partially finished basement, thoughtfully laid out to easily accommodate additional family living if desired. This contemporary home features a spacious 2699 sq ft on the main floor, complemented by an expansive 2910 sq ft in the basement, totaling an impressive 5609 sq ft of living space. The striking front facade welcomes you into a home where open spaces and natural light are paramount. The main floor is an entertainer's delight, featuring a gourmet kitchen with a large island, a spacious dining area, and a cozy living room that seamlessly connects to outdoor living spaces.

Upstairs, the private bedrooms provide serene retreats, including a luxurious master suite with a generous walk-in closet and a spa-like ensuite. The basement extends the home's living area, perfect for a media room, home gym, or additional guest suites, offering flexible ample space for family and guests. The Chynoweth Plan combines elegance with practicality, making it an ideal choice for families seeking a stylish and adaptable home.

  • Square Footage: 5609 sq ft (Main: 2699 sq ft, Basement: 2910 sq ft)
  • Bedrooms: 4 (+ optional basement bedrooms)
  • Bathrooms: 3.5 (+ optional basement bathrooms)
  • Features: Modern design, open-concept living, gourmet kitchen, large windows, master suite with spa bath, bonus room, finished basement, 3-car garage.
