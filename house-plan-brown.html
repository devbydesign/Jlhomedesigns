About The Brown Plan

The Brown Plan presents a fresh, modern aesthetic combined with highly functional living spaces. This design emphasizes natural light and open connections, making it ideal for both daily living and entertaining. The front elevation showcases clean lines and inviting curb appeal. Inside, the main floor features a fluid layout with a well-appointed kitchen, a comfortable dining area, and a spacious living room that extends to the serene backyard.

The upper level offers private retreats with generously sized bedrooms and well-designed bathrooms, ensuring comfort and privacy for everyone. The finished basement provides additional versatile space, perfect for a media room, home office, or extra guest accommodations. With a strong focus on contemporary living and elegant detailing, The Brown Plan is crafted to offer a refined and comfortable home environment.

  • Square Footage: Approximately 3650 sq ft
  • Bedrooms: 4 (+ optional basement bedroom)
  • Bathrooms: 3 (+ optional basement bathroom)
  • Features: Modern aesthetic, open-concept living, ample natural light, spacious bedrooms, finished basement, 2-car garage.

Price: Get this custom plan for just 50 cents per square foot!
