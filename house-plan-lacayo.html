About The Lacayo Plan

The Lacayo Plan presents a striking modern facade, setting the tone for a home designed with both elegance and everyday functionality in mind. The main floor is a testament to open-concept living, featuring a spacious and inviting layout that seamlessly integrates the living, dining, and kitchen areas. A large, central island in the gourmet kitchen serves as a focal point, ideal for both meal preparation and casual gatherings. Expansive windows throughout ensure the main living spaces are bathed in natural light, enhancing the contemporary feel and offering a connection to the outdoors.

The thoughtfully designed main floor also includes well-appointed private spaces, ensuring comfort and tranquility. The lower level offers a versatile finished basement, providing ample additional square footage for a variety of uses such as a media room, home office, or additional bedrooms. This adaptability makes The Lacayo Plan an excellent choice for families seeking a stylish and flexible home that can evolve with their needs, combining sophisticated design with practical living.

  • Square Footage: Approximately 4400 sq ft
  • Bedrooms: 3 (+ up to 4 optional basement bedrooms)
  • Bathrooms: 2.5 (+ up to 2 optional basement bathrooms)
  • Features: Modern aesthetic, open-concept main floor, gourmet kitchen with large island, abundant natural light, versatile finished basement, spacious bedrooms, 2-car garage.
