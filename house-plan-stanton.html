About The Stanton Plan

The Stanton Plan offers a timeless appeal with its classic architectural details and a layout that beautifully balances traditional charm with modern functionality. The inviting front facade hints at the spacious and thoughtfully designed interiors within. The main floor features elegant common areas, including a formal dining room and a cozy living room, complemented by a large, well-equipped kitchen perfect for family gatherings. A convenient study or home office is also located on this level.

Upstairs, the private quarters consist of a luxurious master suite with a generous walk-in closet and a spa-inspired bathroom, alongside three additional bedrooms, each providing ample space and comfort. The finished basement expands the living possibilities, offering a versatile recreation area, a potential fifth bedroom, and additional storage. The Stanton Plan is designed for those who appreciate enduring style and a home built for lasting memories.

  • Square Footage: Approximately 4950 sq ft
  • Bedrooms: 5 (+ optional basement bedroom)
  • Bathrooms: 3.5 (+ optional basement bathroom)
  • Features: Classic design, formal dining, spacious kitchen, main-floor study, luxurious master suite, finished basement, 3-car garage.

Price: Get this custom plan for just 50 cents per square foot!
