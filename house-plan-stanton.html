About The Stanton Plan

The Stanton Plan offers a grand and sophisticated rambler design with an unfinished basement, blending timeless elegance with exceptional functionality. The stately front facade showcases classic architectural details and a welcoming entrance. Inside, the main floor spans 2424 sq ft and features an elegant great room that flows into a spacious, open-concept kitchen with a large island and high-end finishes, perfect for both everyday living and entertaining. A well-placed sunroom, followed by several thoughtfully designed bedrooms, finishes off the main floor, providing versatile spaces for living and special occasions.

The expansive unfinished basement provides an additional 2512 sq ft, offering significant space ideal for a customizable recreation area, home gym, or extra bedrooms, allowing for endless possibilities for personalization. With a total of 4936 sq ft, The Stanton Plan is designed for those who desire a distinguished home with ample space and refined details, ready to be tailored to their specific needs.

  • Square Footage: 4936 sq ft (Main: 2424 sq ft, Basement: 2512 sq ft)
  • Bedrooms: 3 (+ up to 3 optional basement bedrooms)
  • Bathrooms: 2.5 (+ up to 2 optional basement bathrooms)
  • Features: Rambler style, unfinished basement, stately front, elegant great room, gourmet kitchen, sunroom, luxurious finishes, 3-car garage.
