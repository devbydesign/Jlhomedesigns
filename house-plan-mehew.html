About The Mehew Plan

The Mehew Plan is a thoughtfully designed rambler, offering a harmonious blend of traditional elegance and modern comfort. This plan uniquely features a versatile bonus room above the three-car garage, utilizing attic truss living space to maximize square footage. The inviting facade exudes curb appeal, welcoming you into a home where functionality meets sophisticated design. The main floor boasts a functional layout with a welcoming foyer, a dedicated home office, and an open-concept living area. The heart of the home is a generously sized kitchen, complete with a walk-in pantry and a large island, perfect for culinary adventures and casual dining, flowing seamlessly into the family room.

With an upper square footage of 494 sq ft, a spacious main floor of 2219 sq ft, and an expansive basement totaling 2508 sq ft, this home offers a grand total of 5221 sq ft. The serene master suite provides a private oasis with a luxurious bathroom and ample closet space. Additional bedrooms offer comfort and privacy for family members, accompanied by well-appointed bathrooms. The basement adds significant versatile living space, ideal for a recreation room, home gym, or an additional guest suite, enhancing the home's overall functionality. The Mehew Plan is crafted to provide a truly comfortable and adaptable lifestyle.

  • Square Footage: 5221 sq ft (Upper: 494 sq ft, Main: 2219 sq ft, Basement: 2508 sq ft)
  • Bedrooms: 5 (+ optional basement bedrooms)
  • Bathrooms: 3.5 (+ optional basement bathroom)
  • Features: Rambler style, bonus room over 3-car garage, home office, walk-in pantry, open-concept living, luxurious master suite, finished basement.
