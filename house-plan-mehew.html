About The Mehew Plan

The Mehew Plan is a charming and spacious home designed for families seeking both elegance and practicality. Its inviting facade features classic architectural elements that exude curb appeal. Inside, the main floor boasts a functional layout with a welcoming foyer, a dedicated home office, and an open-concept living area. The heart of the home is a generously sized kitchen, complete with a walk-in pantry and a large island, perfect for culinary adventures and casual dining, flowing seamlessly into the family room.

Upstairs, a serene master suite awaits, offering a private oasis with a luxurious bathroom and ample closet space. Three additional bedrooms provide comfort and privacy for family members, accompanied by well-appointed bathrooms. The finished basement adds significant versatile living space, ideal for a recreation room, home gym, or an additional guest suite, enhancing the home's overall functionality. The Mehew Plan is crafted to provide a harmonious blend of traditional charm and modern amenities for a truly comfortable lifestyle.

  • Square Footage: Approximately 4100 sq ft
  • Bedrooms: 5 (+ optional basement bedrooms)
  • Bathrooms: 3.5 (+ optional basement bathroom)
  • Features: Traditional design, home office, walk-in pantry, open-concept living, luxurious master suite, finished basement, 2-car garage.
