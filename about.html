Our Story

Crafting Dreams, One Plan at a Time.

Who We Are

JL Home Designs is a family business started by Jared Lesser. He led our company for over 20 years creating stunning house plans and winning awards. Our designs have even been featured in Parade of Homes. After he passed away in 2022 his family continued the business, lead by his sister Alicia Stephens. We are a small but passionate team dedicated to transforming your vision of a perfect home into a tangible plan. With years of experience in architectural design and a deep understanding of modern living, we specialize in creating custom house plans that are not only beautiful but also highly functional and affordable.

Our Philosophy

We believe that quality homes begin with attention to detail - whether it's designing efficient use of space with extra closets or that perfect kitchen and butler's pantry you've always dreamed of. We want to help you design the home you love, without breaking the bank. Our commitment to quality craftsmanship and innovative design drives us to offer one of a kind house plans at an unbeatable price of just 50 cents per square foot. We pride ourselves on transparent pricing, exceptional customer service, and a collaborative design process that keeps you in control.

Our Team

Behind every successful design is a dedicated team. Our architects and designers are experts in their field, continuously exploring new trends and technologies to provide you with the best possible solutions. We are here to guide you through every step, from initial concept to final blueprint, ensuring your complete satisfaction.
